THE WEDDING

MAY 8TH, 2024 | Domaine d’Essendiéras, Dordogne, France

Where

At the heart of green Périgord, the Domaine d’Essendiéras welcomes you on over 360 hectares of beautiful French countryside. An exceptional place, steeped in history, it was the property of great personalities such as academician André Maurois and businessman Sylvain Floirat. Let yourself be surprised by its two castles of the sixteenth and nineteenth centuries, its views and exceptional perspectives.

OUR STORY

EVERY GREAT LOVE

STARTS WITH A GREAT STORY

Our story began in the early days of 2017, right in the heart of a Canadian winter. The crisp air and polar chill kept us indoors so we ended up spending hours talking about our cultures and where we're from. After three amazing years living together in Montreal, we decided to move back to Paris at the end of 2019. We just knew we were meant to be together.

That's when we decided to get married. The twists and turns of 2020 meant we couldn't celebrate this moment with everyone back then. But we're determined to make up for it and share this important step with all of you. So, we wholeheartedly invite you to join us for this special occasion!
